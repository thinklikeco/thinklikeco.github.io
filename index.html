Hi there.

I'm Chase Oliver, a freelance product designer residing in the great state of Maine. This site is where I share some of my work along with a few thoughts on design. You can find me elsewhere on the web tweeting, ranting, and posting works in progress.

My design philosophy is simple: do whatever it takes to make useful products people use. That often includes idea validation, interaction and visual design, copywriting, user testing, development, product positioning, and user adoption.

Over the years I've delivered useful products for companies small and large. But whether I'm working for someone else or myself, I love teaming up with smart people to deliver something so instinctive it couldn't be imagined any other way.
