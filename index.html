Hi there.

I'm Chase Oliver, product designer from the coast of Maine. This site is where I share some of my work along with a few thoughts on design. You can find me elsewhere on the web tweeting, ranting, and posting works in progress.

My design philosophy is simple: do whatever it takes to make useful products people use. That often includes user research, interaction and visual design, copywriting, usability testing, development, product positioning, and user adoption.

Over the years I've delivered useful products for ambitious companies large and small. But whether I'm working for someone else or myself, I love teaming up with incredible people to deliver something so instinctive it couldn't be imagined any other way.

Thanks for stopping by,

Chase

Research, design, test, and iterate

Your business starts and ends with your users. I research their behavior, combine their needs with the needs of your business, then design, test, and iterate until a clear MVP is defined.

I work at Barkly as part of the team crafting the best endpoint security product on the market, but like any true product person I'm always interested in chatting about compelling ideas.
